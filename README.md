# Olist E-commerce SQL Business Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the Brazilian Olist E-commerce dataset using SQL to answer real business questions related to sales performance, customer behavior, product categories, payments, reviews, and delivery performance.

The project demonstrates practical SQL skills for data analysis, including joins, aggregations, filtering, grouping, ordering, and business insight generation.

---

## Tools & Technologies

- SQL
- Jupyter Notebook
- SQLite
- GitHub

---

## Business Questions Answered

- What are the top 10 product categories by revenue?
- Who are the top 10 sellers by revenue?
- Which states generate the highest revenue?
- Which states have the highest number of orders?
- Which payment methods are used most frequently?
- How are customer review scores distributed?
- Which product categories generate the highest sales?

---

## Key Insights

- Beauty & Health products generated the highest revenue.
- São Paulo (SP) contributed the largest share of revenue and orders.
- Credit Card is the most frequently used payment method.
- Most customers gave high review scores, indicating strong customer satisfaction.
- Revenue is concentrated among a relatively small number of top sellers.
